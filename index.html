Projects: Programming

DUOL

DUOL is an app that was started at YorkUHacks 2018, and is currently under development.

Using accelerometer data from the phone, our group of 5 created a new way to face off against your friends. Two people on the same network can engage in blade-to-blade combat, taking turns attacking and defending to outplay their opponent. Moves or gestures are done by physically swinging your phone, in the same way you would with an actual sword. Players can also put their reactions to the test with an "endless" game mode.

DUOL was built in Android Studio (Java). Right now, our goal is to publish this to the Google Play Store for anyone to download and play for free.

Bus Routing Application*

*placeholder name - This app is also under development.

For the beginning part of the year, our school bus company couldn't find available busses for us. Very often it was late, and we usually were met with a new driver each day. The driver wasn't familiar with the route, and often times we took a suboptimal route.

Although traditional routing services can be generated automatically, bus stops are usually placed by hand, which can take up a lot of time and effort depending on the number of houses.

Our Node.js server retrieves data from HERE's Geocoding and Routing APIs, and our client renders the map and route. It only uses plain HTML/CSS for testing purposes, but we will upgrade it to use Bootstrap.

Hackathons + Contests

F Speaking: the F stands for Fix

My group's submission for Hack the 6ix 2018 was a web application that could help people practice their public speaking. Using Google's Speech to Text API, we compared someone's speech with their original text and suggest things they could improve on. We built the application with Node.js, using p5.js and CSS to draw objects on screen.

Insurance Informatics

At Hack The North 2018, my group made a program that could read insurance data and dynamically display demographic data using dc.js. These graphs can be interacted with and filtered to a user's selection.
